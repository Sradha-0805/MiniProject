About Us

The manual work involved in calculating the mess bill in hostels is tedious as it is time-consuming, ambiguous, and causes calculation mistake.The aim of this project is to provide an easy, efficient, and accurate way to run a hostel mess. The mess bill of the inmate is a customized one based on their attendance. The Mess Drive helps the inmates to access all the functionalities of the mess without having to visit the mess physically.
